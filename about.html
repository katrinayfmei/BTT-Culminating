About Us

Here at ReforestNATION 2.0, we feel that community provides an invaluble source of power and strength, which is why we want you to get to know us. Trust also plays a crucial role in getting things done efficiently, and climate change is certainly an issue we need to solve quickly. Although we might not get to meet each and everyone one of you, we hope that sharing a little more about ourselves will motivate you to suppport our cause and to have a positive impact on the world around you.

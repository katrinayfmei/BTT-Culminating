Future Projects

Here at Reforestnation, we have plans to regrow forests, make safe spaces. and build a community. These projects are still in a work in progress. If you would like to help us, sponsor or partner with us. Please email us at reforestnation2.0@gmail.com

Vietnam

ietnam is still recovering from the Vietnam War. The United States sprayed over 20 million gallons of Agent Orange to purposely damaged forests. Agent Orange is a very strong chemical pesticide. Over one million suffered from health problems from Agent Orange. Most of the forests and land are heavily damaged by Agent Orange.

India

India has a high rate of deforestation and has a lot of crime. Some parts are unsafe for women and children. We plan to build a stronger community, provide education and plant trees.


Colombia

Colombia is a relatively safe country, however, there are many communities with high rates of crime. Colombia has a fast rate of deforestation due to illegal logging. In 2017 Colombia lost over 425kilohectares lands of trees. We plan to plant trees in Colombia and provide support to build a strong community.

Kenya

We want to help Kenya with both of their problems. Environmentally Kenya is suffering from, deforestation, desertification, water shortages and degraded water quality. With the peace aspect, there are many Street Crimes in many communities. We hope to build a strong relationship in many communities to provide safety.

Why Should You Donate to Us?


Our non profit organization has three goals; To become a globally involved non profit organization, to reduce and bring awareness to the amount of c02 in the air and to be recognized as a reliable solution to combat climate change. These are goals that humanity should believe in and want to help us obtain. Reforestnation2.0 is a trustworthy organization. We have received many awards for our work and will continue to do good. We are set on our mission and will help people. Reforestnation is not limited to one focus but joins three different issues and has a single solution. As we help people in Pakistan regrow their forests and obtain peace the project will become more stable. Once this happens we will be able to move our Treepods to a new location and start the process all over again. One day we will be able to help people worldwide.

Donation Usage


The majority of the donations will go to food and board for the volunteers. Donations allow us to continue to provide food and board for the amount of volunteers we currently have. This will help us keep the Treepods functioning and the community involved. The rest of the donations will be split between necessary repairs and expansion of Reforestnation. We not only provides the citizens with Treepods and a safe space, we also give the community benches, tables and once we have collected enough donations, play structures. This makes the space more usable and enjoyable. We want to build communities, obtain peace, and reduce the carbon dioxide in the air. This is only possible with your generations.

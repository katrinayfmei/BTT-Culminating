Who We Are

Our three wonderful founders, Maya Rochon, Katrina Mei and Rio Flanagan, all originate from Toronto, Ontario. When they met at social justice high school Ursula Franklin Academy, they had no idea they would be changing the course of human existence. However, motivated by the pressing issue of climate change and the inequalities it causes world wide, they founded a company that was set to make history. Each founder brings a variety of skills and degrees to the table and helps to create a truly unique and capable team.

Maya Rochon
@mayarochon

Maya Rochon serves as the campaign maganer for ReforestNATION 2.0 and is an extremely organized person. She attented the University of Toronto where she earned her degree in environmental studies; as well as MIT, where she powered through to receive degrees in chemical engineering and environmental technology. In her spare time, Maya enjoys skiing, baking cupcakes and advocating for women's rights.
Katrina Mei
@katrina_mei_

Katrina Mei is ReforestNATION 2.0's primary researcher, an invaluble and tiring position. Armed with a degree in biochemistry from Harvard University and bachelors in geology and business from McGill University, she provides new perspectives and keeps all our projects up to date. Some of Katrina's hobbies include running marathons, making stain glass and lending her time to varius charities.
Rio Flanagan
@rio.0034

As ReforestNATION 2.0's project manager, Rio Flanagan's schedule is always full. During her time at Cambridge University, she conducted groudbreaking research on the effects of deforestation to native wildlife. She has also earned degrees in forestry and behavioral science from the University of Calgary. Rio likes to play hockey, eat lollipops and adopt and care for homeless, injured animals.

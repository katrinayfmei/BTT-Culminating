Why Should You Volunteer?


Experience

Volunteering for reforestnation2.0 on our Pakistan project will be an experience like no other. You will meet new people and help communities. You will mainly be planting trees, keeping the space clean and teaching and engaging citizens around the area. As a volunteer you will make new relationships, learn new things and be immersed in Pakistan's historic culture. While volunteering in Pakistan you will gain new skills including botany and communication skills.


Resume

Volunteer work looks extremely good on a resume. Many employers will not hire people without field experience. Volunteering at Reforestnation2.0, a reputable non-profit organization shows employers that you are a dedicated, hard working person. It also shows that you are able to get along with others and commit to projects. Volunteering will expand your network, making you future connections. All in all volunteering will only improve your career and resume.

    Accommodations

    Volunteers will be given room and bored. We have purchased a 3 story building filled with 15 rooms. Each room contains 2 beds. Our building is a simular style to university residancy. The building contains a kitchen and a bathroom on each floor. Food will be purchased and made by our volunteer chef, Ramone Tear. He just graduated from culinary school and needs excpeirince before he starts working in resturaunts. There is a 2 week meal rotation for all three meals in the day and accomidations will be made for differnet dietary restrictions. All the food is protien based and is meant to help the volunteers get through the day.

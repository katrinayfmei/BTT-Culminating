Plan of Action

We will start our project in the capital city of Pakistan, Karachi. We will be purchasing 20 Treepods and placing them in a smaller community in Karachi called Sultanabad. Reforestation will be providing the community with seating where they can peacefully communicate. Light so citizen can gather safely and saplings for people to plant. The plan is for community members to plant and regrow a forest while our Treepods are absorbing c02. Once a forest has begun to grow we will move our trees and begin the process again with a new community.





Government Involvment

People may assume that it would be difficult to work with pakistan because it is a war torn country. The pakistani government has already recognized and are taking action against their deforestation problems. Because we share the same goal Reforestation believes that it will be easy to work with them. Additionally our project will provide volunteer opportunities for students worldwide which will bring positive attention to the country. Our shared goal and projects benifits makes our Non- Profit Orginization one of the only companies that could partner with a difficult government or country.

Community Building

At reforestation we pride ourselves in helping communities grow and support each other. We provide citizens with a safe gathering space where the Treepods have been implemented which ensures that community members can freely communicate with one another. Caring for the Treepods and rebuilding a forest gives people in the area a common goal. This will also encourage the younger generation to bond in a fun, interactive and useful space. We hope that this unites the next generation and will make Pakistan a more peaceful country in the future. Reforestation benefits communities in many ways, creating lifelong bonds, uniting the community and introducing a peaceful future to Pakistan.
